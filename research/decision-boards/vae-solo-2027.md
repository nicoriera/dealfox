# VAE Solo 2027 — Decision Board

## User profile

- Location: Anglet, France
- Main uses:
  - going surfing;
  - solo rides;
  - family rides alongside the Moma E-Longtail.
- Existing fleet:
  - Moma E-Longtail for family mobility;
  - current personal bike;
  - Sandra's former bike, potentially for resale.
- Purchase horizon: 2027
- Key preferences:
  - design matters strongly;
  - pleasure of riding matters strongly;
  - maintenance and support remain important;
  - the future bike should complement the cargo bike, not duplicate it.

## Product direction

The target is not a second cargo bike.

The target is a stylish, enjoyable and versatile electric bike that can:

- carry surf-related gear;
- handle coastal paths and urban riding;
- remain fun for solo use;
- accompany family rides comfortably;
- offer a credible service network.

## Current strategic view

Three product archetypes should remain in the shortlist:

1. **Design-first lightweight VAE** — best for solo rides and daily desire.
2. **Versatile Bosch VTC** — best for support, range and utility.
3. **Electric gravel** — best for sport and mixed surfaces.

The final choice should only happen after real-world testing because design preference and riding feel are difficult to score from specifications alone.

## DealFox learning

The same user can have very different priorities depending on the role of the product in the household.

For the cargo bike, value and family function dominated.

For the personal bike, design and riding pleasure become first-class decision criteria.
