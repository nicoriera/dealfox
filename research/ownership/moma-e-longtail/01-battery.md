# Moma E-Longtail — Battery notes

## Known facts

The Moma E-Longtail uses a removable lithium-ion battery:

- Voltage: 48 V
- Capacity: 13 Ah
- Energy: 624 Wh
- Status: integrated and removable

Moma also sells an official compatible battery separately:

- Product: Lithium battery 48 V E-MTB / E-Bike
- Price observed: 399.99 EUR
- Compatibility includes: E-LONGTAIL
- Availability observed: in stock at the time of discovery

## Interpretation

This is a positive ownership signal because a replacement battery is visible and purchasable from the manufacturer.

However, this does not prove long-term availability.

## Confirmed strengths

- Official replacement battery exists.
- Replacement price is competitive compared with many e-bike brands.
- Battery is shared across several Moma models, which may improve future availability.
- Removable battery supports easier charging and theft prevention.

## Unknowns

- Battery cell manufacturer.
- BMS quality.
- Expected cycle count.
- Long-term availability over 5 years.
- Warranty process for battery failure.
- Real loaded range with two children around Anglet.

## Usage concerns

Beach usage raised a practical concern:

- removing the battery reduces theft risk;
- carrying it to the beach can be inconvenient;
- battery should not be left in direct sun or on hot sand;
- battery should be kept shaded and protected from sand and salt.

## DealFox learning

A visible replacement battery is not the same as proven long-term support.

DealFox must separate:

- confirmed availability today;
- estimated long-term availability;
- battery quality;
- warranty confidence;
- daily usability.
