# Assembly feedback

## Real user observation

The assembly is significantly harder than expected.

## Observations

- The bike is heavy.
- Assembly is easier with **two people**.
- Having additional tools beyond the supplied kit is recommended.
- This is not a 20-minute beginner assembly.

## Recommendation for future buyers

Plan:
- 2 people
- 1 to 2 hours depending on experience
- Allen keys, torque wrench if available, pedal wrench, pump.

## DealFox learning

Assembly difficulty should become a scored criterion because it directly impacts first ownership experience.
