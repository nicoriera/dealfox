A recent French second-hand listing shows a **Moustache Lundi 20.3** at **2,500 EUR** in good condition.

**Buy index:** 92/100  
**Recommendation:** Buy after inspection

### Why it matters

- premium Bosch-based family cargo platform;
- strong French dealer and workshop network;
- good resale potential compared with online-only brands;
- price now close to mainstream new longtails;
- potentially significant value if family accessories are included.

### Required checks

- exact model year and version;
- mileage;
- Bosch diagnostic and remaining battery capacity;
- battery cycle count if available;
- invoice and frame number;
- maintenance history;
- condition of brakes, drivetrain, wheels, stand and rear rack;
- accessories included: safety bars, cushions, footboards, bags, front rack;
- confirmation that the bike is not linked to theft or finance.
